About Sibaleone Metal Minting

This website showcases the process of minting copper coins and bars using blacksmith methods. From melting the copper to stamping designs, discover the craftsmanship involved in creating these metallic treasures.

Blacksmithing has a rich history, and minting coins and bars requires precision, heat, and skill. Here, you can add your own photos and videos to personalize and share your experiences.
