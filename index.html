About SIBALEONE Metal Minting

I am a blacksmith with a passion for working with metal in all its forms. Whether it’s steel, copper, brass, or something more unusual, I enjoy transforming raw materials into meaningful, durable creations. I am open to a wide variety of projects—functional, decorative, experimental—and I’m always eager to learn new techniques and explore new ideas. If you have a vision, a concept, or even just the seed of an idea, I would love to bring it to life with you. Feel free to reach out and let’s create something unique together.
